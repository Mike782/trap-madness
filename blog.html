How I created this website in one day

Mike Bennett Jr. April 22, 2019 124 Comments

Hey, today I will be telling you guys to step by step how I created this Perfect Modern Design website in 24 hours. The first thing I did was look into Trap Madness. I wanted to know what I was getting my self into. I wanted to know what type of team I was working for/with. Once I got to know the Trap Madness team I then begin watching/listening to some of there tracks, and reading there comments to get to know them better. Once I got to know the team and their community better, I begin collecting the data I need for the website. After I got all the data needed, I begin the actual coding part. I created an index.html file and I worked for 3 hours. After I got all the coding done, I implemented photos/icons into the site. Once I was done I sent the beta version to Trap Madness team, and they liked it. I then begin putting more hours into the website, and implementing new features like the "Notification" system, blog page, and the partner page.

Please note, I still update this website weekly. This blog post is just saying how I released the beta version of the site in 24 hours.
